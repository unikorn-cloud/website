What is Unikorn?

Unikorn is a suite of applications that manage the life-cycle of on-demand cloud resources. Its core responsibility is the provisioning of Kubernetes workload clusters in a simple to use, cloud provider agnostic way.

Cloud-Native

Developed exclusively for deployment and execution on Kubernetes. Embraces best-practices of stateless services, automatic scaling and high availability.

Unikorn integrates seemlessly with standard Fluentd logging, Prometheus monitoring, and OpenTelemetry solutions for observability.

Secure

Draws upon industry standard secrity protocols such as OIDC and Oauth2 to provide secure identity services with enterprise ready direct integration with Google and Microsoft identity services.

You can integrate with your own OIDC complicant identity service too.

Open Source

Fully open, and licensed under the Apache-2 software license. We welcome all suggestions and collaborations.

Unikorn uses Open Source components such as ArgoCD and Cluster API.
